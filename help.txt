*** Shortcuts ***

** Global **

Arrows          - Navigation
[]              - Cycle between menus/panes
Enter           - Select item
q               - Quit

** Folder View (Middle pane) **

m               - Move thread to a folder (deleted to delete)
s               - Sort threads by unread/date

** Thread View (Right pane) **

r               - Reply to an email
i               - Open image attachments
